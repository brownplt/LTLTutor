Review: Until (U)

In LTL, x U y means that x holds at every step until y holds. Importantly, x does not need to be false when y is true. Instead, think of it this way: y eventually becomes true (at some future point), and up until that point, x continuously holds true.
For example, the following trace satisfies the formula x U y even though x is true at the same time as y. 

 
            flowchart LR;
            n1["x & !y"]-->n2["x & y"];
            n2["x & y"]-->n3["!x & !y"];  
            n3["!x & !y"]-->n3["!x & !y"];
