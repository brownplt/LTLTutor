Be mindful of operator precedence Tip

In the absence of parentheses, it is important to be wary of operator precedence and associativity rules.

  • Unary operators (X, F, G, !) have the highest precedence and will bind tightly to the formula that immediately follows them.
  • Binary operators (&,|,->,<->,U) are left associative. This means that in expressions involving multiple operators, the operations are grouped from left to right.
    For instance, an expression like a | b & c -> d would be interpreted as ((a | b) & c) -> d.
